About Us

Our Story & Mission

We are on a mission to connect people with great food and empower local businesses to thrive in the digital age.

Who We Are

Food Shops was founded in 2020 with a simple yet powerful vision: to make great food accessible to everyone while helping local restaurants and food vendors grow their businesses. What started as a small startup in San Francisco has grown into one of the most trusted food delivery and marketplace platforms in the country.

We believe that food is more than just sustenance — it is an experience, a connection, and a celebration of culture. Our platform brings together passionate food lovers, talented chefs, and dedicated delivery partners to create a seamless food delivery ecosystem that benefits everyone.

Our Vision: To become the most loved food marketplace platform, known for reliability, quality, and community impact.

Our Mission

At Food Shops, our mission is threefold:

  • For Customers: To provide a fast, reliable, and enjoyable food ordering experience with access to diverse culinary options from local restaurants and vendors.
  • For Vendors: To empower restaurants and food businesses with technology, visibility, and logistics support to reach more customers and grow their revenue.
  • For Delivery Partners: To create flexible earning opportunities with fair compensation, safety support, and growth potential.

Why Choose Food Shops

Lightning Fast Delivery

Our optimized logistics network ensures your food arrives hot and fresh, with an average delivery time of just 30 minutes.

Trusted & Secure

We verify every vendor and delivery partner. Your payments and personal data are protected with bank-level security.

Community First

We prioritize local businesses and create economic opportunities in every community we serve.

Quality Assured

Every partner restaurant meets our quality standards. We regularly audit vendors to ensure food safety and hygiene.

Wide Selection

From street food to fine dining, we partner with diverse vendors to offer something for every taste and budget.

Innovation Driven

We continuously invest in technology to improve the ordering experience, delivery efficiency, and partner tools.

Benefits for Everyone

For Customers

  • Access to hundreds of local restaurants and food vendors in one app
  • Real-time order tracking and accurate delivery estimates
  • Multiple secure payment options including cards, wallets, and cash
  • Exclusive deals, loyalty rewards, and personalized recommendations
  • 24/7 customer support for any issues or inquiries

For Vendors

  • Expand your customer reach without additional marketing costs
  • Easy-to-use dashboard for managing orders, menu, and inventory
  • Data insights and analytics to understand customer preferences

For Delivery Partners

Our Values

Customer Obsession: We start with the customer and work backwards. Every decision we make is guided by what is best for our users.

Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency in everything we do — from pricing to partnerships.

Innovation: We embrace change and continuously seek better ways to serve our community.

Inclusivity: We celebrate diversity and create opportunities for businesses and individuals from all backgrounds.
