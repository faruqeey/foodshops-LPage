Account Management

Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your data. Follow the instructions below to permanently delete your FoodShops account and associated data.

Important: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All your order history, saved addresses, payment methods, rewards points, and personal information will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

How to Delete Your Account

FoodShops provides multiple ways to delete your account, in compliance with Apple App Store requirements and data protection regulations:

  1. Using the Mobile App (Recommended)

    Open the FoodShops app → Go to Profile → Tap Settings → Select Account → Tap Delete Account → Confirm your decision by entering your password. Your account will be scheduled for deletion immediately.

  2. Using the Website

    Log into your account at foodshops.com → Go to Account Settings → Click Delete Account → Follow the verification steps. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is processed.

  3. Contact Customer Support

    If you cannot access the app or website, contact our support team directly. We will verify your identity and process your deletion request within 5 business days.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your account and all personal information will be permanently deleted
  • Your order history and transaction records will be anonymized or deleted per legal requirements
  • Any active subscriptions or recurring orders will be canceled
  • Your rewards points and promotional credits will be forfeited
  • You will be logged out of all devices immediately
  • Any pending orders will be canceled and refunded

Data Retention Notice

While we delete your personal data promptly, some information may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes:

  • Transaction records may be retained for 7 years as required by tax and financial regulations
  • Anonymized data may be retained for analytics and business improvement purposes
  • Records related to disputes or legal matters may be retained until the matter is resolved

For complete details, please review our Privacy Policy.
