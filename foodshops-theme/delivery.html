Why Deliver With Us

Flexible Work, Great Earnings

FoodShops delivery partners enjoy the freedom to work on their own schedule while earning competitive pay. Whether you want to work full-time or just a few hours a week, we have opportunities that fit your lifestyle.

Our delivery partners are the backbone of our service. We invest in your success with training, support, and the best technology to make every delivery smooth and efficient.

  • Work whenever you want — full flexibility
  • Competitive per-delivery pay plus tips
  • Weekly direct deposits to your bank account
  • Bonuses and incentives for top performers
  • Insurance coverage while on delivery

How It Works

Getting Started is Easy

From application to your first delivery, the process is designed to be quick and hassle-free. Here is how it works:

  • Apply Online: Fill out the delivery partner application with your basic information and vehicle details.
  • Background Check: We conduct a quick background check to ensure safety for our community.
  • Orientation: Complete a brief online orientation covering app usage, safety protocols, and best practices.
  • Get Your Kit: Receive your FoodShops delivery bag, uniform, and any necessary equipment.
  • Start Delivering: Log into the app, go online, and start accepting delivery requests in your area.
