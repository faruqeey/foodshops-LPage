How It Works

Simple Steps to Get Started

Getting your restaurant on FoodShops is quick and straightforward. Our onboarding team will guide you through every step of the process.

  • Apply Online: Fill out the vendor application form with your business details and menu information.
  • Get Verified: Our team reviews your application and verifies your business licenses and food safety certifications.
  • Menu Setup: We help you set up your digital menu with photos, descriptions, and pricing.
  • Start Receiving Orders: Once approved, you will start receiving orders from customers in your area.
  • Grow Together: Use our analytics and marketing tools to optimize your menu and increase sales.
